Favicon picks

Collection of Beauty — eight directions to choose from

To install your pick, copy the chosen SVG to src/app/icon.svg. Next.js picks that up automatically at /icon and wires the <link rel="icon"> headers for you. For Safari pinned tabs, also copy it to src/app/apple-icon.svg.

All icons are 64×64 SVG, single-stroke where possible, tested to stay legible at 16×16 (actual browser-tab size, shown leftmost in each card). Toggle the theme above to see each on a dark tab strip.
